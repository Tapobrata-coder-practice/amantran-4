## 🎨 PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES

### 1. Hero Background Image (`hero-bg.jpg`)

**What to Shoot:**
- Wide shot of your property exterior OR
- Beautiful interior lounge area OR
- Scenic view from property

**Technical Requirements:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- Orientation: Horizontal (landscape)
- Lighting: Golden hour (early morning/evening) for warm tones
- Composition: Leave space in center for text overlay

**Photography Tips:**
- Use a wide-angle lens (or phone panorama mode)
- Shoot during golden hour (6-7 AM or 5-6 PM)
- Ensure buildings/horizon are level
- Avoid harsh midday shadows
- Include some sky (rule of thirds)

**Bad Examples to Avoid:**
❌ Blurry/pixelated images
❌ Dark/underexposed shots
❌ Cluttered/messy backgrounds
❌ People blocking main view
❌ Harsh overhead sun creating hard shadows

**Good Examples:**
✅ Well-lit exterior with clear sky
✅ Clean, inviting entrance
✅ Balanced exposure (not too bright/dark)
✅ Professional composition

## 📱 SMARTPHONE PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS

**Don't have a professional camera? No problem!**

Modern smartphones (iPhone, Samsung, etc.) are excellent for this. Follow these tips:

### Before Shooting:
1. Clean your phone lens thoroughly
2. Enable HDR mode in camera settings
3. Use grid lines to align shots (Rule of Thirds)
4. Lock focus by tapping on subject
5. Avoid using digital zoom (move closer instead)

### While Shooting:
1. Hold phone horizontal (landscape mode)
2. Keep phone steady (use tripod or lean against wall)
3. Take 5-10 shots of each scene
4. Vary angles slightly
5. Check focus and exposure before moving on

### Lighting Rules:
- ☀️ Golden Hour: Best time (sunrise/sunset)
- 🌤️ Overcast Days: Great for even lighting
- 💡 Indoor: Turn on ALL lights + open curtains
- 🚫 Avoid: Midday harsh sun, fluorescent-only lighting

## 🎯 POST-PROCESSING

After taking photos, improve them before uploading:

### Free Tools:
- **Snapseed** (Mobile app) - Easy adjustments
- **Canva** (Web/Mobile) - Resize & basic editing
- **Photopea** (Web) - Free Photoshop alternative
- **GIMP** (Desktop) - Free professional editor

### Quick Edits (Do These):
1. **Crop** to exact dimensions needed
2. **Brightness** - Increase if too dark (not too much)
3. **Contrast** - Slight increase for pop
4. **Saturation** - Slight boost for vibrant colors
5. **Straighten** - Fix tilted horizons
6. **Sharpen** - Subtle sharpening

### Advanced Edits (Optional):
- Remove small distractions (trash cans, signs)
- Adjust white balance (make warmer)
- Enhance shadows/highlights
- Reduce noise (for low-light shots)

## 🗜️ IMAGE COMPRESSION

**CRITICAL: Compress all images before upload!**

### Why Compress?
- Faster website loading
- Better Google rankings
- Less bandwidth usage
- Better mobile experience

### How to Compress:

**Method 1: TinyPNG (Recommended)**
1. Go to https://tinypng.com
2. Upload your image
3. Download compressed version
4. Use compressed version for website

**Method 2: Squoosh (Advanced)**
1. Go to https://squoosh.app
2. Upload image
3. Choose "WebP" format (better compression)
4. Adjust quality to 80-85%
5. Download

**Target File Sizes:**
- Hero image: < 500KB
- Feature images: < 300KB
- Small images: < 200KB
- Favicon: < 10KB
