Our Story

Amantran Guest House was born from a vision to create more than just a place to stay—we wanted to craft an experience. Founded by a family deeply rooted in the cultural tapestry of Birbhum, our establishment honors the rich heritage of Tarapith while embracing modern comfort and style.

The name "Amantran" means "invitation" in Bengali, reflecting our core belief that every guest is an honored visitor invited into our home. This philosophy permeates everything we do, from the warm welcome at our entrance to the careful attention given to every detail of your stay.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of hosting pilgrims seeking spiritual solace, families creating memories, couples celebrating special moments, and travelers discovering the hidden gems of West Bengal. Each guest's story becomes part of our own, and we're honored to be part of your journey.
